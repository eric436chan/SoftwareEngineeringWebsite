My order hasn't arrived. What do I do?

 Do you offer free shipping?

We offer free shipping on orders that total more than $70.00. Only available to to orders with addresses within the US.

 Do you deliver to PO boxes?

We currently do not deliver to PO Boxes because we can only deliver to street addresses. To make sure we get the right delivery to the right person we ask that you sign to recieve your order.

 I got a wrong order or it was damaged, what do I do?

All of our packages are carefully wrapped in the warehouse before being shipped out. Please check the package and see if there is any signs of damage, or if the package has been opened during shipping time. Send us an email here and we will assist you in getting your order.

 How can I track my order?

 Where do you ship?

What is Jack's return policy?

Jack's Apparel provides returns on wrong orders, faulty or damaged items within the first 30 days of the order date.

 When will I recieve my refund?

You will receieve your refund once we receive and verify that the item sent is the one returned. You may recieve partial refund if the item has been tampered or damaged.

 How will I receive my refund?

Your refund will be sent to the bank account that you used to order the item.

 What should I know before I retrun?

Verify that the order being sent back is the one you ordered. Item must be new, in unwashed condition.
